Frequently Asked Questions

How scientific is astrology?

While astrology is not a science in the modern sense, it is based on precise celestial observations and thousands of years of accumulated experience. We use modern celestial mechanics for accurate calculations to ensure planetary position accuracy. Astrology is more of a study about the quality of time and psychological archetypes.

Why do different websites show different horoscopes?

Different astrologers have varying interpretation styles and focuses. We employ traditional astrological methods combined with modern psychological astrology, emphasizing personal growth and self-awareness rather than simple fortune-telling predictions.

What effects do retrograde planets have on us?

Planetary retrograde is a visual effect, but in astrology it represents periods of introspection and re-evaluation. Different planetary retrogrades have different influences: Mercury retrograde affects communication, Venus retrograde affects relationships, Mars retrograde affects motivation and action, etc.

What's the difference between Rising sign and Sun sign?

The Sun sign reflects core personality and life goals, while the Rising sign reflects external presentation and others' first impressions of you. The Rising sign requires accurate birth time for calculation, while the Sun sign only needs the birth date.
