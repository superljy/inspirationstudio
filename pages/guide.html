Frequently Asked Questions

Why do query results vary each time?

Astrology is based on real celestial movements, and planetary positions change every moment, so your fortune analysis will change accordingly. This reflects the dynamic nature of cosmic energy and is one of the core characteristics of astrology.

What do high and low fortune scores mean?

Fortune scores reflect your overall energy state and action potential during the current period. High-score periods are suitable for taking initiative and starting new projects; low-score periods are suitable for introspection, rest, and preparation. The key is learning to adjust your behavioral strategies in different energy cycles.

How can I better utilize lucky elements?

Lucky elements are tools for enhancing your personal energy field. You can wear lucky colors on important occasions, refer to lucky numbers in key decisions, arrange seating according to lucky directions, etc. The key is to consciously integrate these elements into daily life.

How does zodiac compatibility analysis apply to real relationships?

Compatibility analysis provides a framework for understanding interaction patterns between different zodiac signs. High compatibility sign combinations often understand each other more easily, while low compatibility combinations require more patience and understanding. Remember, real relationship success depends more on both parties' efforts and growth.
