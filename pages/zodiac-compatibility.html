Compatibility Factors

  • Elemental Harmony: Signs of the same element often understand each other naturally
  • Complementary Elements: Fire + Air, Earth + Water create balanced relationships
  • Planetary Rulers: Venus and Mars positions affect romantic compatibility
  • Modalities: Cardinal, Fixed, and Mutable signs have different relationship approaches
  • House Positions: Where planets fall in each person's chart matters
